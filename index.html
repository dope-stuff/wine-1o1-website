Story of US

Ever stood in a wine shop, feeling kinda clueless? That was me! Totally into wine, but those fancy terms? Not so much. A true enthusiast at heart, but lost in a storm of jargons - Merlot, Malbec, Dry, full-bodied? What did it all mean?

Diving into the wine-selling world, It felt overwhelming with all the experts around. But conversations with equally perplexed café owners sparked an idea.

Why should wine be a lecture when it's meant for laughter and memories?

That's how Wine101 came to life - a community where wine isn't about fancy words but about fun, inclusivity, and a shared journey of discovery. No judgment, just good vibes. Cheers to keeping it simple and sippable!
